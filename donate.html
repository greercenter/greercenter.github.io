Donate

The Carol J. Greer Community Learning Center fosters the growth, development, and empowerment of youth residing in economically depressed areas of Lansing. Through a comprehensive range of programs, resources, and opportunities, the GCLC inspires, educates, and uplifts its members, equipping them with the skills, confidence, and support needed to overcome challenges and reach their full potential.

Your donation to the GCLC benfits Lansing youth living in poverty and provides you with the maximum tax deduction allowed by law, since the GCLC is a 501(c)(3) organization. Giving is easy using using any of the methods listed below.

By Mail

Mail a check with Greer Center on the memo line to St. Stephen's Community Church, 1007 Kimberly Dr, Lansing, MI 48912.

On-line to St. Stephens Community Church

On the Giving page of the website for the St. Stephens Community Church UCC:

  • Scroll down and click Donate.
  • Fill the amount of your contribution into the text box labeled Community Outreach Fund: Greer Center Fund (4th from the top).

Directly into the GCLC Endowment Fund

The Carol J. Greer Community Learning Center has established an endowment fund at the Community Foundation. Current gifts of cash and other assets to our fund can provide you with a tax deduction. We also encourage you to name the Greer Center Endowment Fund as a beneficiary in your will or estate plan.

Both current and deferred gifts help to ensure the GCLC will benefit generations to come. For more information about our endowment fund and how giving to our endowment benefits you and the GCLC, read our Greer Center Endowment Pamphlet.

To donate to our endowment fund please contact: Community Foundation +1 517-272-2870.
