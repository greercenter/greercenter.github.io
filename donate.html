Donate

The Carol J. Greer Community Learning Center fosters the growth, development, and empowerment of youth residing in economically depressed areas of Lansing. Through a comprehensive range of programs, resources, and opportunities, the GCLC inspires, educates, and uplifts its members, equipping them with the skills, confidence, and support needed to overcome challenges and reach their full potential.

Your donation to the GCLC benfits Lansing youth living in poverty and provides you with the maximum tax deduction allowed by law, since the GCLC is a 501(c)(3) organization. Giving is easy using using any of the methods listed below.
