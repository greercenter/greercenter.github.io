Carol J. Greer Community Learning Center Partners

It is with immense appreciation and deep respect that we recognize and honor the invaluable contributions of our partners and affiliates. Partners support the GCLC with monetary contributions. Affiliates provide in-kind donations and educational programs. All play an integral role in the realization of a shared vision: a future where every individual has the chance to reach their full potential and make a meaningful difference in their lives and the community. The dedication and generosity of GCLC's partners and affiliates fuel our efforts to pave a pathway to success for the youth of Lansing.

AllOccasionsAprons

-- ENHANCING LIVES THROUGH THE ART OF THE APRON --
AllOccasionsAprons of the St. Stephen's Community Church Sewing Guild offers unique, handmade items, including custom aprons, themed collections, and lap scarves and robes. Proceeds support the Greer Community Learning Center and Computer Lab with educational programs for Lansing-area youth and adults.

Capital Region Community Foundation

The mission of the Capital Region Community Foundation is to lead philanthropic solutions that build vibrant communities in Ingham, Eaton and Clinton counties. It accomplishes its mission by serving donors to meet their personal charitable goals, supporting area nonprofits with grants and coaching, and leading innovative projects that drive community growth.

Uplift Our Youth Foundation

The Uplift Our Youth Foundation empowers youth to achieve success and live healthy productive lives. It provides funding to smaller and under funded non-profit organizations and programs that directly impact youth in Ingham, Eaton, and Clinton counties. It awards grants and sponsorship to causes, organizations, and programs that have a primary focus of youth achievement and success with an emphasis on serving “at-promise” youth, diversity inclusion, education, culture, employment and life skills management.

City of Lansing

The City of Lansing (CoL) generously supports the GCLC through its grants program. CoL grants target organizations and programs that provide services in helping Lansing residents meet their basic needs, employability development, health, mental health, and life skills.

Michigan State University

Michigan State University (MSU) provides educational programs and assists with field trips for GCLC youth. Many thanks to the MSU groups that regularly volunteer with us: MSU College of Engineering, MSU Computer Science and Engineering Department, and MSU Tennis Club.
